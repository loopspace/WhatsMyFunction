What's My Function?

Your goal is to guess the hidden function. You can either put in values to see what comes out, or put in a value and predict what will come out.

To demonstrate that you have guessed the function, you need to make five correct predictions.

You start with 100 points and you use these points as follows:

  • 5 points: to put in a value and see what comes out.
  • 10 points: for an incorrect prediction.
  • 5 points: if you put in a value or make a prediction using a value that is not in the domain of the function.

Any points left over will be added to your score at the end.
